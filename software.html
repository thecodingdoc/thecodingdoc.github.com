Software

  • GOUtil a command-line tool part of the FunSet  pipeline for performing and visualizing Gene Ontology enrichment analysis
  • CanBind a pipeline to identify genes significantly mutated in cancer
  • molBLOCKS a suite of programs to break down small molecules into fragments and analyze their distribution
  • fcsampling a program to generate function-constrained samples of genes that match a target distribution
  • EasyMIFs & SiteHound tools to identify and characterize protein binding sites using structural information
